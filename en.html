About Us

Every Rupiah You Give, Changes Lives

In remote villages — far from decent facilities, far from attention — many of our brothers and sisters still struggle just to access clean water, basic education, and hope. Yayasan Trah Manshur exists to bridge the gap between your compassion and those who need it most.

Not just temporary aid. We build sustainable programs in social, educational, and religious fields — measurable, transparent, and with real impact. Together with hundreds of beneficiaries across 3 villages, we prove that collective compassion can drive great change.

Join us. Donate. Become part of this story of change — because one action of yours today can be a light for many families tomorrow.
